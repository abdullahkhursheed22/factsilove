Ribbon worms feed using a proboscis. When they encounter prey they shoot it out to inject toxins and digestive juices.

![[worms-earthworm.jpg]]

Earth worms have 5 ribbon-like hearts.

![[worms-bloodworm.jpg]]

Blood worms have fangs made of copper. This is the only animal I have heard of that has a body part **entirely** made of metal.
